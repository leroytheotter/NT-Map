Getting Started

This website provides an interactive exploration of a handful of New Testament Events. Feel free to explore the region. Pan. Zoom. Click. Enjoy.

Navigation

There are multiple ways to navigate the CivilScott New Testament Map.


Zooming
You can either zoom in and out with the wheel on your mouse or by clicking the +/- buttons in the top left corner of the map. --->
Panning
Click and hold the left mouse button to pan. Simply drag the map the direction you'd like it to go.
Map Type
Change the map from a street map to a satellite image map using the drop down box in the lower left hand corner. --->
Events
Besides panning from one event to the next, you can jump to the location of specific events using the drop down list immediately below the map. --> Clicking on events in the drop down will make all other events disappear. To see them again either click "Show all Events" in the drop down list or click the refresh button at the top of the page.

Pop-Up Bubbles

Clicking on events will reveal additional information about the event including:
  • Title
  • Date
  • Scriptural Reference
  • Description
  • Beautiful Image

If you look really close you might even find a pop-up bubble that includes a link to additional information of that site. (Hint: It might be by a river.)
Events such as trips or missions are represented as lines. Click anywhere on the line to see more information.
